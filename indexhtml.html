Seattle City Trip

This trip to Seattle is designed to be very accessible and friendly, as it doesn't require driving. This makes it ideal for those who may be unable to drive due to various reasons. If you're able to drive, that's fantastic, but if not, that's perfectly fine too. Plus, it's budget-friendly, with minimal costs for those on a tight budget.

Trip Plan

In this trip, we'll explore various areas across Seattle, encompassing a mix of high-spending and budget-friendly destinations. While some attractions may involve higher costs, the majority of our itinerary consists of free or low-cost activities, ensuring an accessible and enjoyable experience for all.

  • Chinatown:food
  • West Seattle:Alki Beach
  • Spaceneedle(park visit if budget limited)
  • Northgate:shopping(skip if budget limited)
  • University of Washington:Seattle campus(blossom during spring)
